01. Finance Protocol.
Fungi is a decentralized finance (DeFi) protocol that connects crypto managers and retail investors through non-custodial investment vehicles known as DeFunds.
Decentralized Funds (DeFunds) are on-chain investment vehicles that allow crypto managers to perform non-custodial asset management strategies for investors using the best DeFi protocols.
